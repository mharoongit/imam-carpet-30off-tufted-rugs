# Gemini / Veo Video Prompt — Tufted Rugs Eid Sale (23s)

> Use with reference clip (≤10s slice) + this prompt. Target output: 23-sec vertical 9:16 ad for Eid 2026 30% off campaign.

---

## Master Prompt (paste into Gemini Veo)

```
A 23-second cinematic vertical (9:16) product video for a premium Pakistani rug brand "Imam Carpets". Subject: hand-tufted wool-and-viscose rugs in contemporary patterns, large generous sizes, photographed in a warm naturally-lit Karachi showroom and modern living-room set.

STYLE: cinematic, soft golden-hour daylight, shallow depth of field, warm earthy color grade (deep terracotta, cream, muted gold), slow deliberate camera moves. Premium and tactile, not flashy. Reference: high-end interiors brand films (RH, West Elm) with a Pakistani warmth.

PACE: slow, confident. Each shot 2–3 seconds. No cuts faster than 2 seconds. No zooms, no shaky cam.

SHOT LIST (in order):

1. (0–3s) Slow top-down dolly across a fully unrolled large hand-tufted rug on a polished wood floor. Subtle pile texture visible. Soft daylight from window-left.

2. (3–6s) Extreme close-up: human hand brushing across the rug pile. Wool fibres bend and spring back. Soft sheen catches the light. Tiny dust motes in sunbeam.

3. (6–10s) Macro detail of the rug edge — yarn loops pushed through backing cloth, the hallmark of hand-tufted construction. Tilt-shift focus pulls along the edge.

4. (10–14s) Wide shot: rug anchoring a modern Karachi living room — low sofa, brass side table, plant, evening light. Camera pushes in slowly. Empty room, no people.

5. (14–17s) Side-angle shot of a stack of three rolled rugs leaning against a showroom wall. Warm tungsten light. Faint "Since 1952" engraved brass plate visible on the wall.

6. (17–20s) Overhead shot of the rug centred under a coffee table set for Eid — dates, ceramic cups, a single candle. No people. Eid mood without clichés.

7. (20–23s) Close-out shot: clean cream background, the rug rolled neatly with a tied ribbon, soft shadow. Space for end-card text.

ON-SCREEN TEXT (burned in, bold sans-serif white with thin black drop shadow, max 5 words per card):

- 0.0–2.5s (centre): "HAND TUFTED RUGS" with smaller line below "Made in Pakistan"
- 2.5–6.0s (centre, huge, deep-red underline on "30% OFF"): "30% OFF" / "Eid Sale — All 40 designs"
- 6.0–10.0s (lower third): "Wool + viscose" / "Loop by loop. By hand."
- 10.0–13.5s (lower third): "70+ years of handmade rugs" / "Imam Carpets, since 1952"
- 13.5–17.0s (lower third): "Free delivery all Pakistan" / "Karachi — same day"
- 17.0–20.0s (centre, red): "Ends 26 May — Midnight"
- 20.0–23.0s (centre, big): "SHOP NOW" / "imamcarpets.com"

AUDIO: soft instrumental — slow oud and light tabla, low BPM, no vocals. Royalty free. Volume sits under the visuals.

CAMERA: real lens feel (35mm/50mm equivalent), gentle handheld stabilisation, no drone. No people on screen except a single anonymous hand in shot 2.

NEGATIVE PROMPTS (avoid): no text spelling errors, no warped letters, no people's faces, no children, no animals, no logos other than "Imam Carpets", no Western models, no overly saturated colours, no fast cuts, no spinning rugs, no AI-flicker on geometric patterns, no machine-loom shots, no flying carpets, no cheesy sparkles, no neon, no cartoonish typography.

FORMAT: 9:16 vertical, 1080×1920, 23 seconds exact, 24fps cinematic.
```

## How to Use With Reference Clip
1. Trim existing 23s clip to its best 8–10 sec slice (a slow pan over the rug pile usually anchors style best).
2. Upload trimmed clip + paste master prompt.
3. If Gemini outputs <23s, generate 2 takes (12s + 12s) and stitch in CapCut. Hold matching colour grade.
4. Regen any clip where text is misspelt — never let warped letters ship.

## Pakistan-Specific Guardrails
- Living room set must read as Pakistani upper-middle, not generic Western. Brass, dates, low seating, soft fabrics.
- Daylight = morning/evening Karachi light, not harsh noon.
- Avoid prayer mat or religious iconography — keep Eid tone subtle (dates, candle, ceramic).
